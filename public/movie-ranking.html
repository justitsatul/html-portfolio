The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

The reason why this movie is amazing is because of the plot twists that grab your attention along the whole course of the movie.

Spider-Man : Across the Spider-Verse

The animations, the graphics, the story, the character development, all of these make this movie a must watch.

Zodiac

This movie is about a serial killer and his tricks. Robert Downey Jr. and Jake Gyllenhaal play brilliant roles in this thriller.
